# My Favorite Movie

## Overview
My favorite movie is **"The Wind Rises"** by *Studio Ghibli*.  
It’s a beautifully animated historical drama about dreams, love, and perseverance.

---

## Why I Love It
> "The Wind Rises" teaches us to follow our dreams while facing reality.

### Key Points
1. Stunning animation and art style
2. Deep and meaningful story
3. Beautiful musical score
4. Inspiring characters

### Favorite Scenes
- The flight scenes with breathtaking visuals
- Moments between Jiro and Naoko
- The ending that evokes hope and reflection

### Fun Facts
- The movie is loosely based on the life of Jiro Horikoshi, an aircraft designer.  
- Many scenes reflect Miyazaki’s love of aviation.  
- The title comes from a poem by Paul Valéry.
